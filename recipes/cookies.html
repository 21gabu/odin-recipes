Chocolate Chip Cookies

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened: This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe starts with two sticks of butter creamed with white and brown sugars. The blend of sugars creates a perfectly balanced flavor.

  • 1 cup white sugar: Sugar adds sweetness to the cookies.

  • 1 cup brown sugar

  • 2 eggs: Eggs add moisture and act as a binding agent, which means they help hold yhe dough together.

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract: Vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor of the chocolate chip cookies.

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda: Baking soda acts as a leavener, which means it helps the cookies rise.

  • 2 teaspoons hot water: A bit of water creates steam as it bakes, working with the baking soda to puff the cookies up.

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt: A pinch of salt enhances the flavors of the other ingredients, but it won't make the cookies taste salty.

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour: All-purpose flour helps create gluten,which add structure to the cookie dough.

  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips: Of coure, you'll need semisweet chocolate chips! you can use dark or milk chocolate chips if you prefer.

  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional): walnuts are optional, but they add nutty flavor and a welcome crunch.

Directions

  1. gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.

  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (150 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.

  3. Beat eggs one at a time.

  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.

  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.

  8. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
